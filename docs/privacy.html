Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Studio Sentinel ("the app") is developed by GovMed AI. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data Collection

Studio Sentinel does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The app does not have a backend, does not use analytics services, and does not track users.

Local Network Communication

Studio Sentinel communicates exclusively with the Studio Sentinel Agent running on your local network. All data (CPU, memory, thermal, and process metrics) stays within your local network and is never sent to the internet.

Data Stored on Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • A pairing token stored in the iOS Keychain to authenticate with your Studio Sentinel Agent
  • Agent IP addresses and ports saved in UserDefaults for reconnection
  • Alert rules and app preferences saved in UserDefaults

None of this data leaves your device.

In-App Purchases

Studio Sentinel offers an optional Pro upgrade via Apple's In-App Purchase system. All purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

Studio Sentinel does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting services.

Children's Privacy

Studio Sentinel is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
